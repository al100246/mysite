Crochet for Beginners :)

If you're on this page, you are absolutely an absolute beginner ... no worries!
This page will prvide you with all of the essential knowledge you need to get started on your crochet journey!
However, before we get started, may I point out that all images on this page are credit to @lilthings!

Lets get started with a slip knot - all projects will start with either a slip knot or a magic ring, slip knots for working in rows and magic rings for working in rounds.
To make a slip knot, hold your yarn in your right hand, and bring your left hand around it ( fig.1 ). Twist it, then with your thumb and finger pull the yarn tail through the loop ( fig. 2 )
Now you have a slip knot!
